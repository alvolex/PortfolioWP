Moon Rock Caf\u00e9 is a fast-paced Overcooked inspired game where the player has to manage a lot of responsibilities in a space-restaurant. Your main goal is to juggle a lot of different tasks such as seating customers, taking their order & making sure they don’t order something they’re allergic to, getting the orders to the kitchen and finally, when the food is ready, delivering that meal to the correct alien!<\/p>\n

My responsibilities in the project<\/h3>\n
